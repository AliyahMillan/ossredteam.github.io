Bugcrowd University at CSUF

Bugcrowd University is the first ever security / hacking event conducted by Offensive Security Society. During the event, students have opportunity to either hack in a fictitious university or hack into a live target selected by Bugcrowd. The event was host on April 20, 2019 from 12 to 9 PM. During the event, we have both competitions and workshops. Participating in competitions will make you eligible for awards such as trophies and potential cash rewards. For workshops, Students get to learn hacking from the start through an OSCP bootcamp. Students also get networking opportunity to talk with security minded people from Bugcrowd and get to understand the security industry through their perspective.
